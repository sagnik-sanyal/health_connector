# Health Records

Health records are individual data points that represent health measurements
at a specific time (instant records) or over a duration (interval records).

## Record Types

- **Instant Records**: Single-point measurements like weight or blood glucose
- **Interval Records**: Duration-based data like sleep sessions or workouts

## Common Properties

All health records include:

- Timestamps (time or start/end time)
- Metadata (source, recording method, device info)
- Platform-specific identifiers
